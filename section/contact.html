Help

Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Check our most common questions below.

How do I download my purchased speeches?

After completing your purchase, you'll receive an email with secure download links. You can also access all your purchases anytime by logging into your account and visiting the "My Library" section. All files are provided in high-quality MP3 format.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa (via STK Push), credit/debit cards, bank transfers, and mobile money through Paystack. All payments are processed securely with industry-standard encryption.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

Absolutely. We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee, no questions asked. If our content doesn't inspire you, simply contact our support team and we'll process your refund within 3-5 business days.

How can I become a Voice of Power speaker?

We're always looking for powerful voices! Visit our Speakers page and click "Apply to Speak" to submit your application. Our team reviews all applications and will reach out within 2 weeks if there's a fit.

Do you offer corporate or group licenses?

Yes! We offer tailored corporate packages for teams of all sizes. These include bulk licenses, team analytics, and custom content curation. Contact our partnerships team at partners@voiceofpower.co for a custom quote.

Is my personal information secure?

Your privacy is our priority. We use bank-level encryption for all transactions and never share your personal data with third parties. You can read our full Privacy Policy for complete details on how we protect your information.
