Frequently Asked QuestionsQuick Answers to Common Questions

What is Voice of Power?

Voice of Power is Africa's premier platform for motivational speeches and inspirational content. We curate world-class speeches from legendary speakers, making them accessible to anyone seeking transformation, growth, and motivation. Since 2020, we've helped over 50,000 people across 12 countries unlock their potential.

Who are the speakers on Voice of Power?

Our speakers include world-renowned motivational speakers, psychologists, entrepreneurs, athletes, and thought leaders from across the globe. Each speaker is carefully vetted by our panel of experts to ensure authenticity, impact, and the ability to create real change. We feature voices from Africa, the US, the UK, and beyond.

How long are the speeches?

Speech lengths vary to fit your schedule. Most range from 20 to 60 minutes, with some shorter "power boost" speeches under 15 minutes and deep-dive sessions extending to 90 minutes. Each speech listing clearly shows its duration so you can plan accordingly.

Can I listen to a preview before buying?

Yes! Every speech includes a free 2-minute preview so you can get a feel for the speaker's style and energy before making a purchase. We also offer a curated selection of completely free speeches for new users to explore the platform.

Is Voice of Power available in my country?

Voice of Power is available globally. Our primary audience is in Africa (Kenya, Nigeria, South Africa, Ghana, Uganda, Tanzania, and more), but users from the US, UK, Europe, and Asia also enjoy our content. All you need is an internet connection to get started.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept multiple payment methods to ensure accessibility:

  • M-Pesa — STK Push directly to your phone
  • Credit/Debit Cards — Visa, Mastercard via Paystack
  • Bank Transfer — Direct bank deposits
  • Mobile Money — Airtel Money, Tigo Pesa, and more via Paystack

All transactions are processed through Paystack, a PCI-DSS compliant provider trusted by thousands of businesses worldwide.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level SSL encryption for all transactions. We never store your full card details on our servers — all payment processing is handled securely by Paystack. Your financial information is protected by the same standards used by major banks and e-commerce platforms.

Will I receive a receipt or invoice?

Yes. A detailed receipt is automatically sent to your email immediately after purchase. For business or corporate purchases, you can request a formal invoice by contacting our support team at support@voiceofpower.co.

Can I pay in my local currency?

Currently, all prices are displayed in Kenyan Shillings (Ksh). However, Paystack automatically handles currency conversion for international cards. We plan to support multi-currency display in the near future to make pricing even clearer for our global audience.

What happens if my payment fails?

If your payment fails, don't worry — no money will be deducted from your account. You'll see an error message explaining what went wrong. Common causes include insufficient funds, network issues, or incorrect card details. You can retry the payment immediately or try a different payment method.

How do I download my purchased speeches?

After completing your purchase:

  • Go to "My Library" in your account dashboard
  • Find the speech you want to download
  • Click the download icon (downward arrow)
  • The MP3 file will save to your device's default download folder

You can also access download links from the confirmation email sent after purchase.

What file format are the speeches in?

All speeches are delivered in high-quality MP3 format (320kbps), ensuring crystal-clear audio on any device. MP3 is universally compatible with smartphones, tablets, laptops, car stereos, and virtually all audio players. We also provide transcripts in PDF format for Power Pack and Elite subscribers.

Can I listen offline?

Yes! Once you download a speech, you can listen offline anywhere — on a plane, in the gym, during your commute, or anywhere without internet. Offline listening is available on all paid plans. Simply download the speeches you want while connected to Wi-Fi, and they'll be available whenever you need them.

How many times can I download a speech?

Unlimited times. Once you purchase a speech, you can download it as many times as you need. This is helpful if you switch devices, accidentally delete a file, or want to keep backups. Your purchases are permanently tied to your account.

Can I share downloaded files with friends?

Downloaded files are for your personal use only. Sharing files violates our Terms of Service and copyright agreements with our speakers. However, you can easily gift speeches to friends and family through our gifting feature at checkout, which gives them their own licensed copy.

What's the difference between Starter, Power Pack, and Elite?

Starter (Ksh 500/speech): Buy individual speeches one at a time. Perfect for trying out specific content.

Power Pack (Ksh 4,500/month): Unlimited access to our entire library, HD audio, transcripts, and bonus workshops. Best value for regular listeners.

Elite (Ksh 12,000/year): Everything in Power Pack plus monthly 1-on-1 coaching, exclusive live events, private community access, and priority support. For total transformation.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time with no penalties. Go to Account Settings > Subscription and click "Cancel." Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period. After cancellation, any individually purchased speeches remain in your library permanently.

What happens to my downloads if I cancel?

Any speeches you purchased individually are yours forever, even after cancellation. However, speeches accessed through a subscription plan will no longer be available for new downloads once your subscription ends. We recommend downloading your favorites before canceling if you're on a subscription plan.

Can I switch between plans?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings. Upgrades take effect immediately, and you'll be charged the prorated difference. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

Do you offer family or group plans?

Yes! We offer corporate and group licenses for teams, schools, and organizations. These include bulk pricing, team analytics, and custom content curation. Contact our partnerships team at partners@voiceofpower.co for a custom quote tailored to your needs.

What devices and browsers are supported?

Voice of Power works on all modern web browsers including Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge, and Opera. We also have dedicated mobile apps for iOS (iPhone/iPad) and Android. For the best experience, we recommend keeping your browser and operating system up to date.

Why is my download slow or failing?

Slow downloads are usually caused by unstable internet connections. Try these steps:

  • Switch to a stable Wi-Fi connection
  • Pause other downloads or streaming activities
  • Clear your browser cache and try again
  • Use the mobile app for more reliable downloads

If problems persist, contact our support team and we'll send you an alternative download link.

How do I update my account password?

To update your password:

  • Log into your account and go to Account Settings > Security
  • Click "Change Password"
  • Enter your current password, then your new password twice
  • Click "Save Changes"

If you forgot your password, click "Forgot Password" on the login page and follow the reset instructions sent to your email.

The audio is skipping or cutting out. What should I do?

Audio issues are typically caused by network instability or browser conflicts. Try these solutions:

  • Download the speech for offline playback
  • Close other browser tabs and applications
  • Try a different browser
  • Update your audio drivers
  • Restart your device

If the issue continues across multiple speeches, please contact support with details about your device and browser.

How do I report a bug or technical issue?

We appreciate your help in making Voice of Power better! To report a bug:

  • Email us at support@voiceofpower.co
  • Include your device type, browser/app version, and a description of the issue
  • If possible, attach screenshots or screen recordings

Our technical team typically responds within 24 hours and prioritizes critical issues affecting multiple users.
