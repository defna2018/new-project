Our Story

From a Single Idea to 50,000+ Transformed Lives

Voice of Power began in 2020 when our founder, Righan Okello, realized that access to world-class motivational content was limited to those who could afford expensive seminars and coaching programs.

What started as a small collection of recorded speeches shared among friends quickly grew into a movement. Today, we curate and deliver powerful motivational speeches from the world's most influential voices directly to your device.

Our mission is simple: make transformational content accessible to everyone, everywhere, at any time.
