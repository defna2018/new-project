Getting Started

How do I create an account?

Creating an account is simple. Click "Get Started" on the homepage, enter your email and create a password. You'll receive a confirmation email — click the link and you're in!

What devices can I use to listen?

You can listen on any device with a web browser, or download our mobile app for iOS and Android. All speeches are compatible with smartphones, tablets, laptops, and desktop computers.

Do I need an internet connection?

For streaming, yes. But once you download a speech, you can listen offline anywhere — on a plane, in the gym, or during your commute. Downloads are available on all paid plans.

Purchases & Payments

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept M-Pesa (STK Push), credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard), bank transfers, and mobile money through Paystack. All transactions are encrypted and secure.

Read More

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level SSL encryption and never store your full card details. All payments are processed through Paystack, a PCI-DSS compliant payment provider trusted by thousands of businesses.

Read More

Can I buy speeches as gifts?

Yes! During checkout, select "Send as Gift" and enter the recipient's email. They'll receive a personalized message and instant access to the speech. Gift purchases are also eligible for our 30-day guarantee.

What is the difference between plans?

Starter lets you buy individual speeches. Power Pack gives you unlimited access to our entire library monthly. Elite includes everything plus 1-on-1 coaching and exclusive live events. Compare plans on our Pricing page.

Downloads & Access

How do I download a speech?

After purchase, go to "My Library" in your account. Click the download icon next to any speech. Files are delivered in high-quality MP3 format and can be saved to any device.

Read More

Can I share my downloads with others?

Downloads are for personal use only and tied to your account. Sharing files violates our Terms of Service. However, you can gift speeches to friends and family through our gifting feature.

Read More

What if my download fails?

No worries — you can re-download any purchased speech unlimited times from your library. If you continue having issues, contact our support team and we'll send you a fresh download link.

Read More

How long do I have access to my purchases?

Forever. Once you buy a speech, it's yours for life. Even if you cancel a subscription, any individually purchased speeches remain in your library permanently.

Account & Settings

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password" on the login page, enter your email, and we'll send you a secure reset link. The link expires in 24 hours for your security.

Read More

How do I update my email address?

Go to Account Settings > Profile. Enter your new email and click "Save Changes." You'll need to verify the new address before it becomes active.

Read More

Can I delete my account?

Yes. Go to Account Settings > Privacy and click "Delete Account." This will permanently remove your data. Note: any purchased speeches cannot be recovered after deletion.

How do I manage my subscription?

Visit Account Settings > Subscription to view your plan, update payment details, upgrade, downgrade, or cancel. Changes take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.
