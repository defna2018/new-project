Our Refund Policy

At Voice of Power, we stand behind the quality and impact of every speech we offer. This Refund Policy outlines the terms under which you can request a refund for purchases made on our platform.

1. Eligibility for Refunds

You are eligible for a full refund under the following conditions:

  • 30-Day Window: Your refund request is submitted within 30 calendar days of the original purchase date.
  • Individual Speech Purchases: Any single speech purchased through our platform is eligible for a refund.
  • Subscription Plans: First-time subscribers can request a refund within 30 days of their initial subscription payment. Subsequent billing cycles are not eligible for refunds, but you may cancel anytime to prevent future charges.
  • Gift Purchases: The recipient of a gifted speech may request a refund within 30 days of receiving the gift.

Note: Refunds are processed to the original payment method used for the purchase. If the original payment method is no longer available, we will work with you to find an alternative solution.

2. Non-Refundable Items

The following are not eligible for refunds:

  • Speeches downloaded and consumed in their entirety (over 90% listened) where no technical issues were reported.
  • Corporate or bulk licenses after the 14-day evaluation period.
  • Promotional or free items, including bonus content and giveaways.
  • Refunds requested after the 30-day window has expired.

3. How to Request a Refund

Requesting a refund is simple:

  • Log into your Voice of Power account and navigate to My Orders.
  • Find the purchase you wish to refund and click "Request Refund."
  • Alternatively, email us at refunds@voiceofpower.co with your order number and purchase date.
  • Our team will process your request within 3-5 business days.

You do not need to provide a reason for your refund, though we welcome any feedback that can help us improve.

4. Refund Processing Time

Once approved, refunds are processed as follows:

  • M-Pesa: 1-3 business days
  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-10 business days (depending on your bank)
  • Bank Transfer: 3-7 business days
  • Mobile Money: 1-3 business days

You will receive a confirmation email once your refund has been initiated. If you do not see the refund within the expected timeframe, please contact your payment provider first, then reach out to us for assistance.

5. Partial Refunds

In rare cases, partial refunds may be issued for:

  • Duplicate charges caused by technical errors.
  • Subscription downgrades where the price difference is owed to you.
  • Promotional bundle purchases where individual items are defective.

6. Changes to This Policy

Voice of Power reserves the right to update this Refund Policy at any time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Refund Policy or need assistance with a refund request, please contact us:

  • Email: refunds@voiceofpower.co
  • Phone: +254 712 345 678 (Mon-Fri, 9AM-6PM EAT)
